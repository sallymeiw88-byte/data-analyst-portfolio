# 🏠 Real Estate Commercial Analysis - Andes Capital (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes the commercial performance of **Andes Capital**, focusing on sales trends, property types, and customer recurrence in Bogotá to drive strategic business decisions.

## 🎯 Business Questions
* Which property types generate the highest revenue?
* How has the company grown compared to the previous year (YoY)?
* Which sales channels (Brokers vs. Direct) are most effective?
* What is the customer retention rate based on their first purchase month (Cohort Analysis)?

## 🗂️ Dataset
* **Sales Data:** Transactional records of property sales.
* **Property Dim:** Details about houses, apartments, and commercial spaces.
* **Customer Data:** Buyer segmentation and purchase history.

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills Used
* **Power BI:** Data visualization and dashboard design.
* **DAX:** Advanced measures for Time Intelligence, YTD, and Cohort Analysis.
* **Data Modeling:** Implementation of a **Star Schema** for optimized performance.

## 📊 Key Metrics
* **Total Revenue:** ~$6bn with a **111.14% Year-over-Year growth**.
* **Top Asset:** Houses lead the market in total income.
* **Sales Force:** Brokers (Corredores) drive 72.8% of the total revenue.
* **Retention:** The October 2024 cohort showed a peak in customer recurrence (26.5%).

## ✅ Key Learnings
* Developed complex DAX formulas to calculate business growth and retention.
* Designed an executive-level interactive dashboard for stakeholders.
* Applied Cohort Analysis to understand long-term customer behavior.
* Structured a clean data model using Star Schema principles.
